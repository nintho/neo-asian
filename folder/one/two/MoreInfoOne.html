Lunch Special






Indulge in NEO Asian Kitchen's exclusive lunch special, available between the hours of 12pm and 3pm. Experience a range of delectable entrees to suit every palate, with prices ranging from $10 to $13. Choose from our carefully curated selection of three enticing options and elevate your lunchtime experience.


  • Pho (Beef, Chicken, Vegetables, Tofu)
  • Garlic Noodles (Beef, Chicken, Vegetables, Tofu)
  • Banh Mi (Special Combination, Grilled Beef, Grilled Pork, Grilled Chicken, Tofu)

A selection of four rolls:


  • Egg Roll (Pork)
  • Spring Roll (Vegetables)
  • Summer Roll (Steamed Pork and Shrimp)
  • Grilled Summer Roll (Grilled Pork)

A refreshing beverage selection that spans from sodas to teas, allowing you to complement your meal with the perfect drink of your choice.


Soda


  • Coke
  • Diet Coke
  • Sprite

Tea


  • Hot Green Tea
  • Iced Green Tea


The lunch special at NEO Asian Kitchen is available for dine-in customers, with the option to take it to-go for an additional $1 fee.
