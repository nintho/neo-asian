FAQs


Before reaching out to us, please take a moment to browse our FAQ section to see if your question has already been answered. If you can't find what you're looking for, don't hesitate to Contact us! We're always here to help and will get back to you as soon as possible.



Menu, Nutrition, Allergens

Are there any dairy-free options?

Yes, we do offer dairy-free options! Our menu allows for the substitution of dairy milk with alternative options like lactose-free milk, almond milk, and oat milk for most drinks. Additionally, we have a selection of drinks such as butterfly teas and special teas that are free of any milk products.

Are there any caffeine-free drinks?

Yes, our butterfly teas does not contain any caffeine.

Are there any dish without gluten?

Unfortunately, we do not have any gluten-free options, as most of our dishes contain a minimal amount of gluten.

Do you have any vegetarian or vegan options?

Yes, we do offer vegetarian and vegan options at our restaurant. Our dishes can be customized to accommodate your dietary preferences, as most of our dishes allow you to choose the protein. However, please note that some of our sauces may include animal products such as oyster and fish sauce and honey. Please inform our team of your dietary restrictions, and we will be happy to adjust the ingredients to meet your needs.

Are there any foods free of seafoods?

Although we offer a variety of menu items that do not contain seafood as an ingredient, we cannot guarantee that our food has not come into contact with seafood. Our kitchen uses the same oil to fry all appetizers, including those that contain seafood. We understand the seriousness of food allergies and encourage our customers to inform their server of any allergies or dietary restrictions so that we can accommodate them to the best of our abilities.

Is there a kid's menu?

Yes, we offer a menu specifically for children that includes several options to choose from. For more details on the dishes available for kids, please feel free to ask your server.

Neo Rewards

How do I sign up to get points?

To sign up and earn points, simply provide your phone number at the time of purchase and our system will automatically register you for our rewards program.

How do I earn points?

For every $1, you will receive 1 point.

How do I redeem my points?

To redeem your points, simply inform our team during your purchase that you have accumulated enough points to claim your reward.

Services

Do you offer catering?

Yes, we offer catering services for events and parties. However, we kindly ask that you let us know ahead of time, so we can prepare accordingly.

Do you offer delivery or takeout services?

We offer a convenient in-house delivery service via Menufy, which can be accessed directly on our website. This service covers a 5-mile radius from our location. To expand our delivery options beyond this radius, we also partner with third-party delivery services such as UberEats, GrubHub, and DoorDash.

Do you take reservations?

Our restaurant does take reservations, and you can make them by giving us a call. However, please keep in mind that we may not be able to accommodate your request if we're already at full capacity. Therefore, it's best to let us know ahead of time, so we can prepare accordingly and ensure that we have enough tables available to meet your needs.

Do you offer gift cards?

We offer gift certificates that can be purchased for any amount, and our team members are available to assist you with your purchase. Simply ask one of them, and they'll be happy to help you get started.

Payments

Do you accept Apple Pay?

Yes, we accept Apple Pay.

Do you accept credit cards?

Yes, we accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover.

Do you accept payments over the phone?

Unfortunately, we do not currently accept payments over the phone. We accept payments in person at our restaurant or through our online ordering system.

Refund Policy

Can I get a refund for my purchase?

If you are not satisfied with your purchase, please let us know and we will do our best to make it right for you. We offer refunds on a case-by-case basis depending on the circumstances.
